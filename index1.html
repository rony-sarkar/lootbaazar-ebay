Digital The Quran Read Touch Pen word by word function in Alloy Box Point & Listen

Description:

The Quran Reader Pen is a reciter assisting device which is designed for Muslims/non-Muslims to learn to recite Holy Quran with an actual reciting method through its user-friendly features and interactive guiding program that makes it suitable for all ages. The best tool to read, learn and memorize The Holy Quran.

The pages are layered with invisible coded grids. The Pen Reader optically detects each unique code on the grid and will read the corresponding text in beautiful digital sound. When the Reader pen is pointed at the beginning title of each text, it will read the whole corresponding chapter. This provides a very convenient way to learn and memorise and recite, all in just 3 easy steps – point, listen and recite.

The sound comes through the built-in speaker of the pen, and earphones can be plugged in for a more concentrated and private listening. Simple and easy way for people with reading difficulties, learning disabilities or dyslexia to get immediate word support when they are reciting.

“With this new breakthrough technology, the learning process is accelerated!”

Features:

  • Holy Quran Pen Word-by-word function: Quran Reader to read any page, surah or ayah when touching the pen on any page, passage of text or colour coded function buttons. Quran word-by-word recitation for learning the Quran.
  • Full Quran can be listened to in 5 pre-installed reciter voices (additional Over 19 famous reciters can downloaded in the Digital Quran Pen (Sudais, Menshawi, Gamidi, Basit,), you can download more reciters from the manufacturer’s website, but the voice into pen “DATA” folder, then can read. (Khaleel al Hosary, Abdullah Basfar, Abu Bakr Shatri, M Jabir, Abdur Rahman Sudais, Abdul Basit, Mahir al Muaqyli, Ahmed al Ajmy, Saad Ghamdi, Mohammed Minshavi, Mishary al Efasy, M. Matrood,)
  • Quran translation audio available for over 25 Translations available (English, French, Urdu, Turkish, Farsi, Spanish, Pashto, Somali, Tamil, Kurdish, Maliyalam, Bengali, Chinese, Russian, Deutsch, Portuguese)
  • Can listen to any reciter together with any translation audio with combined mode feature. Voice recording and MP3 player feature.
  • Battery saving features - automatically switches off after 3 minutes of non-use. PC connection using USB port.
  • Internal Li-Ion Rechargeable battery, long time playing, (Digital Quran Reader takes about 1.5hs to be fully charged and 3.5-4hs continuous playing, once fully charged then the light goes off, light in red means in charge).
  • To change translations, adjust volume and speaking speed, please point and refer to the small booklet (same as User Manual), the other way is to point the Icons on the Quran accordingly, long-press the third of Quran reader button to change translations.
